Pre-Market Reading

Read before opening your trading platform

Foundation

I am a disciplined trader. Today I will execute my system with precision.

I take three deep breaths now. I feel my feet on the ground. I am calm, centered, and ready.

My Rules

  • I will only enter trades that meet my system criteria
  • I will set my stop loss before I enter every trade
  • I will honor every stop loss immediately when hit
  • I will let my winners run according to my predetermined exit strategy
  • I will not revenge trade or chase the market

Capital Protection

Every dollar I risk today compounds my future wealth. I protect my capital above all else. A small loss today preserves my ability to trade tomorrow.

Emotional Awareness

When fear whispers "get out early," I will trust my system.
When greed shouts "risk more," I will stick to my position size.
When hope pleads "give it one more candle," I will honor my stops.
When anger demands "get it back now," I will walk away.

Today's Commitment

I place my hand on my heart and commit: I will trade my plan, not my emotions. I will treat each trade as one in a series of thousands. I will be patient, disciplined, and consistent.

The market is opening. I am ready.
